Elif has a BFA from the Fashion Institute of Technology in NYC. She has been an NCIDQ certified Interior Designer since 2008, and is professional member of ASID, as well as a CID in the State of California.

As an Interior Designer, Elif brings a unique perspective to the team. Her in-depth understanding of people and the way they use and interact within different spaces, is what sets her apart. She has been working one-on-one with clients, architects, and multiple trades, designing high-end residential interiors and exteriors, as well as commercial interiors, for the last 15 years. To be successful in the field of interior design, first, one must understand people and what they need, not just what they tell you. This comes intuitively and naturally for Elif.
